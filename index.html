How we estimate your earning potential

The default earnings estimates we display are for entire home listings with a guest capacity of four. Estimates are based on booking data from the last two years for your area and are calculated by multiplying a nightly price by days of occupancy (occupancy rate). The nightly prices used are based on average prices (before expenses, fees and taxes). The occupancy rates used to calculate monthly and annual estimates are based on the top 50% of listings as measured by how often they are booked. The top half of listings is used to illustrate the earning potential for engaged hosts. For the monthly and annual estimates only listings with sufficient availability are considered. Weekly estimates use 7 nights of occupancy. Where you provide information about your potential listing, the estimate we provide reflects that information. Amounts are estimates and not guaranteed. How much you actually make will depend on your availability, price, listing characteristics and amenities, acceptance and cancellation rates, guest reviews, applicable legal restrictions, demand in your area, and other factors.
